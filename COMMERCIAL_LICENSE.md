# Commercial Licensing for FactorySim

## Open Source License (Default)

FactorySim is available under the [GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0)](LICENSE). This means you are free to use, study, modify, and distribute FactorySim as long as:

- You share any modifications under the same AGPL-3.0 license
- If you offer FactorySim (or a modified version) as a network service, you must make the source code available to users of that service
- You include the original copyright notice and license

The AGPL-3.0 is a true open-source license recognized by the Open Source Initiative (OSI).

## When Do You Need a Commercial License?

You need a commercial license if you want to:

- **Embed FactorySim in proprietary software** without releasing your source code
- **Offer FactorySim as part of a closed-source SaaS product** or cloud service
- **Use FactorySim internally** in a large organization that cannot comply with AGPL-3.0 obligations
- **Distribute a modified version** without sharing your changes publicly
- **Remove AGPL-3.0 attribution requirements** from your product

## What Does a Commercial License Include?

- Permission to use FactorySim in proprietary/closed-source products
- No obligation to share your source code modifications
- No AGPL-3.0 network-use requirement
- Priority support and bug fixes
- License terms tailored to your use case

## Pricing

Commercial licenses are offered on a case-by-case basis depending on your use case, company size, and deployment scope. Options include:

- **Single-product license**: Use FactorySim in one commercial product
- **Enterprise license**: Unlimited internal use across your organization
- **OEM license**: Redistribute FactorySim as part of your product
